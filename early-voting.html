📅 Early Voting in Rhode Island

Can’t make it on Election Day? Vote early — no excuse needed in Rhode Island.

✅ Good News: Rhode Island has no-excuse early voting. Any registered voter can cast an in-person early ballot during the early voting period.

How Early Voting Works

1

Go to any early voting location

You must vote at an authorized early voting site, not your regular polling place.

2

Bring your ID

Show your RI Driver’s License, state ID, or other approved photo ID.

3

Sign the poll book & cast your ballot

Your signature will be compared to your voter registration record.
