How It Works

1

Create a Free Account

Join RI First Vote to access all training materials and track your progress.

2

Complete Modules

Watch videos, read guides, and pass short quizzes. Each module is 15-60 minutes.

3

Earn Badges & Certificates

Completing a course unlocks a badge displayed on your profile and earns points for the leaderboard.

4

Apply Your Skills

Sign up for a volunteer shift and put your training into practice in the field!
