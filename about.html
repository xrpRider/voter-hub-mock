🌟 About RI First Vote

Our mission, our team, and why we do this work.

Our Mission

RI First Vote is a nonpartisan civic technology initiative dedicated to empowering every eligible Rhode Islander to participate fully in democracy. We believe voting is not just a right — it’s the foundation of a strong community.

What We Do

🎟️

Voter Registration

We simplify the RI registration process with step-by-step guides, deadline reminders, and direct links to official resources.

🎓

Civic Education

Our Training Academy provides free courses on how voting works, your rights at the polls, and how to get involved in local government.

🤝

Community Building

We connect volunteers, voters, and organizers across RI’s 39 cities and towns to coordinate outreach and drive turnout.

📊

Data-Driven Outreach

Using technology and data, we identify underrepresented communities and bring targeted resources directly to them.
