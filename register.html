📋 Register to Vote in Rhode Island

First time? Moved? Changed your name? Get registered in minutes.

⏰ 2026 Deadlines: Online/Mail registration closes 30 days before the election. In-person registration closes at 9pm on Election Day.

How to Register

💻

Online

Register or update your registration at vote.ri.gov using your RI Driver’s License or state ID number.

Register Online →
📧

By Mail

Download the RI voter registration form, fill it out, and mail it to your local Board of Canvassers before the deadline.

🏛️

In Person

Register at the RI DMV, your local Board of Canvassers, or participating state agencies such as DCYF and DHS.

🗓️

Election Day Registration

Rhode Island offers same-day registration! Register at your polling place or a central location on Election Day until 9pm.

Eligibility Requirements

RequirementDetails
Age18 years old by Election Day (16+ can pre-register)
CitizenshipU.S. Citizen
ResidencyRhode Island resident (no minimum time required)
