Ways to Get Involved

🚪

Door Knocking

Go door-to-door in your neighborhood to register voters and remind them of upcoming elections. We provide scripts, maps, and training.

Get Trained →
📞

Phone Banking

Call registered voters from home or at our offices to confirm registrations and share voting info. Flexible hours — mornings, evenings, weekends.

See Shifts →
📱

Text Banking

Send personalized text messages to voters using our approved platform. Great for tech-savvy volunteers who prefer digital outreach.

Learn More →
📝

Voter Registration Drive

Set up a table at events, schools, or community centers and help people register on the spot. We supply all materials.

Find Events →
🏫

Poll Work

Serve as a poll worker on Election Day. Get paid, help your community, and ensure a smooth voting experience for everyone.

💻

Digital Outreach

Help manage social media, create content, or run digital ads to spread the word about voter registration and elections.
