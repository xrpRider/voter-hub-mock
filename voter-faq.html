❓ Voter FAQ

Everything you need to know about voting in Rhode Island

Registration
How do I register to vote in Rhode Island? +
You can register online at vote.ri.gov, by mail using the National Voter Registration Form, or in person at your local Board of Canvassers, the DMV, or other state agencies. The deadline is 30 days before an election.
What is same-day voter registration? +
Rhode Island allows same-day registration (called "in-person registration") during early voting and on Election Day. You can register and vote at your polling place or early voting site with proof of address.
How do I check if I'm registered? +
Visit vote.ri.gov and use the Voter Registration Lookup tool. You'll need your name, date of birth, and ZIP code to check your registration status.
Voting
Do I need a photo ID to vote? +
First-time voters who registered by mail may need to show ID. Otherwise, you'll sign the poll book and a poll worker will verify your signature. A RI voter ID card (free) is available from the Board of Elections.
What are early voting options? +
Rhode Island offers early voting typically starting 20 days before an election. You can vote in person at designated early voting locations in your city or town. No excuse is needed.
Can I vote by mail? +
Yes! You can request a mail ballot for any reason. Request your mail ballot at least 21 days before the election. Return it by mail postmarked by Election Day or drop it off at your Board of Canvassers by 8 PM on Election Day.
Where is my polling place? +
Use our Polling Place Locator or visit vote.ri.gov to find your assigned polling location based on your registered address.
RI First Vote Program
What is the RI First Vote Voter ID Card? +
The RI First Vote Voter ID Card is a free card we help you get from the RI Board of Elections. It serves as an official photo ID for voting and other purposes. Visit our Get Your Voter Card page to start the process.
How does the volunteer rewards program work? +
Earn points for every civic action - registering voters, phone banking, canvassing, and more. Points convert to badges and appear on our Leaderboard and Wall of Champions.
Is my personal information safe? +
Yes. We take privacy seriously and never sell your data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details on how we handle your information.
